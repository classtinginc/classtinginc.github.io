Products

Class

Class is a place for teachers, students and parents to communicate and to participate. Share and manage classrooms announcements, posts, photos and video contents.

Noticeboard

Teachers deliver announcements to parents and students through PC and smart phone. Pre-schedule notice send out function and confirmation of receipt and re-send function is available.

Post

Stay up-to-date with your class news, recent activities from online communities, and more. Individual can participate with post comments and light-up function.

School

Check class lists in my school and easy subscription of announcements and newsletters to school. Classting is the easiest way to communicate between the school administrator and parents.

Learning

Effectively prepare learning materials for class. Use existing learning materials from various sources, create new assignments, assign tasks to classes, and collect results. Check students' achievements at a glance and analyze their strengths and weak spots.

AI

Classting's AI tutor 'SAM' provides 80,000 questionnaires designed with IRT(Item Response Theory) to assess students' academic achievements in real time. Based on these individual evaluations, 'SAM' recommends personalized learning materials picked from 20,000 video lessons and supplementary tutorials.

Play

Students should have a chance to participate in various classes at this age of learning. Classting provides a variety of after-school activities for students to have fun in learning beyond traditional education system.
